Jiro Ramen

Ingredients

For Soup

  • PRE BOILED PORK BONES 400g
  • PRE BOILED PORK BACKBONE 500g
  • WATER 3L
  • PORK SPARE RIBS 3pcs
  • WATER 500ml
  • PORK FAT 1pc
  • MINCED PORK FAT 200g
  • WATER 500ml

For Noodles

  • WATER 105ml
  • KANSUI 4g
  • SALT 4g
  • FLOUR 250g
  • SEMOLINA 50g

For Topping

  • BOILED PORK FAT
  • CABBAGE
  • BEAN SPROUT
  • GARRIC
  • CHASHU

How to cook

For Soup

  1. Add Pre Boiled Pork Bones, Pre Boiled Pork Backbone, Pork Spare Ribs and Water into a large pot
  2. Start stove at strong fire to boil
  3. Once soup start to boil, remove scum
  4. Switch to low fire and continue to simmer for 90min
  5. Remove Spare Ribs from pot
  6. Add Water 500ml, Pork Fat and Minced Pork Fat into the soup
  7. Change to strong fire
  8. When soup is boiling, cover and continue to boil 40min
  9. After 40min, add more Water 500ml into pot
  10. Cover again and boil 40min more
  11. Remove Pork Fat from soup
  12. Stop fire
  13. Strain the Soup. Soup is done

For Noodles

  1. Mix with Water, Kansui and Salt
  2. Add kansui mix into Flour and Semolina and stir
  3. Use a roller to press into a smooth flat dough
  4. Using a noodle machine, make flat and long roll of dough
  5. For Jiro-Style ramen use thick noodles
  6. Noodles are done
